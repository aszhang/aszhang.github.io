Qizhen Zhang

About

I am now a research assistant at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, building distributed systems to process large-scale graphs, e.g. querying, mining.
Currently, I am developing a general-purpose querying framework, called Quegel, to query on billion scale graphs in real time. Part of this work was accepted by VLDB'16, while another part was accpected by SIGMOD'16.

Before I came to Hong Kong, I was a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia, working on improving Microsoft Azure, one of the largest cloud computing platforms in the world.

You can reach me at qiz.z[at]outlook.com
